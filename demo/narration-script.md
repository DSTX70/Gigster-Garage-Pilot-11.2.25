## Production Notes

### Voiceover Guidelines

**Pace:**
- Moderate speed (150-160 words per minute)
- Clear enunciation
- Energetic but professional tone

**Pauses:**
- Brief pause (0.5s) after scene transitions
- Emphasize key features with slight vocal emphasis
- Natural breathing between sentences

**Tone Adjustments:**
- Scene 1: Welcoming, friendly
- Scenes 2-6: Informative, enthusiastic
- Scene 7: Professional, value-focused
- Scene 8: Impressive, enterprise-grade
- Scene 9: Motivating, call-to-action

### Background Music

**Suggested Style:**
- Corporate/tech background music
- Upbeat but subtle
- 60-80 BPM
- Non-intrusive
- Fade in: 0:00-0:02
- Fade out: 1:28-1:30

**Volume:**
- Music: -20dB to -15dB
- Voiceover: 0dB (primary)
- Sound effects: -10dB (if used)

### Visual Effects

**Transitions:**
- Smooth fades between scenes (0.5s)
- Subtle slide transitions for emphasis
- No flashy effects

**Highlights:**
- Circular highlight for button clicks
- Subtle glow on important UI elements
- Red borders for "Pro/Enterprise" badges

**Text Overlays:**
- Scene titles: Top-left, 2 seconds
- Key features: Bottom-third callouts
- Plan badges: Top-right corner

### Captions/Subtitles

**Format:**
- White text with black outline
- Sans-serif font (Arial, Helvetica)
- Font size: 32pt
- Bottom-center positioning
- 2-line maximum

**Timing:**
- Sync with narration
- 1-2 second display minimum
- Fade in/out (0.2s)
