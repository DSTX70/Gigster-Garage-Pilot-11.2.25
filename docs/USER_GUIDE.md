# Gigster Garage - User Guide

Welcome to Gigster Garage! This guide will help you get started and make the most of the platform.

---

## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

### 1. Create Your Account
Sign up or log in to access your dashboard. First-time users will be guided through a quick onboarding wizard to set up their profile.

### 2. Set Up Your Business Profile
Navigate to **Settings > Profile** to add:
- Your business name and logo
- Contact information
- Default payment terms

### 3. Add Your First Client
Go to **Clients** and click **Add Client** to create your first client record with their contact details.

### 4. Create Your First Invoice
Navigate to **Invoices > New Invoice** to generate a professional invoice. Use the **Net 7/14/30** quick buttons to set common payment terms.

## Core Features

### Dashboard
Your home screen shows:
- **Next Actions Card** - Personalized recommendations for what to do next
- **Quick Stats** - Today's tasks, invoices pending, recent activity
- **Setup Progress** - Steps to complete your account setup

### Tasks
Organize your work with powerful task management:
- Create tasks with **titles, descriptions, and due dates**
- Set **priority levels** (High, Medium, Low)
- Assign tasks to **projects** for organization
- Use **filters** to find tasks by status or priority
- Switch between different **view modes** for your preferred workflow

### Clients
Keep all client information in one place:
- Store **contact details** and company info
- View **invoices and proposals** sent to each client
- Add **notes** about conversations or preferences
- Track **project history** per client

### Invoices
Create professional invoices quickly:
- **Auto-fill** your company details
- Add **line items** with descriptions and amounts
- Use **Net 7/14/30** buttons for quick payment term selection
- **Preview** before sending
- **Download as PDF** or send directly via email

### Filing Cabinet
Store and organize your documents:
- **Upload** files of any type
- **Tag and categorize** for easy searching
- **Search** across all your documents
- **Organize** files in folders

### Time Tracking
Log your work hours:
- **Start/stop timer** for active tracking
- **Manual entry** for past work
- **Assign time to tasks or projects**
- Generate **time reports** for clients

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Check your email and password are correct
- Try the "Forgot Password" link to reset
- Clear your browser cache and try again

**Changes not saving?**
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page and try again
- Look for error messages at the top of the screen

**Invoice not sending?**
- Verify the client's email address
- Check if email integration is configured in Settings
- Try downloading as PDF and sending manually

**Session expired?**
- You'll be redirected to login with a "Session expired" message
- Log in again and you'll return to where you were
